About

This is the IEEE Student Branch of NAIST, built in 2003.

  • Mission

    Be the bridge for learning the edge of technology to NAIST students

    We aim to support students through our events to learn the edge of technology while having fun and making strong international connections!

  • Vision

    Become an exemplary Graduate IEEE SB in Japan!

    We want to bring inspiration and support not only to students from our school, but to those of other graduate schools in Japan! We believe that collaboration is the key!
