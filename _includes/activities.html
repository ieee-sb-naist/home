Activities

These are some of the activities that we hold together with other Student Branches in Kansai Section

English Presentation Competition

Research presentations from students of universities in Kansai section to evaluate and improve english ability

Women in Engineering Symposium

Lectures from female researchers and engineers, and space to interact for female researchers and students

Research Exchange Meeting

Research presentations from students of universities in Kansai section to share research from different areas and universities

Brainstorming Competition

Form groups to practice and improve brainstorming techniques to solve problems
